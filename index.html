Dive in to the world of Cyber Security

Information about the course

We are organising a cyber security course in the shiny city of Mostar, in the beautiful south of Bosnia and Hercegovina where the sun shines the brightest. We will be learning for 10 days the best ways to identify threats, observe them and find the best ways to neutralise them. The days will be filled with the best experiences and adrenaline. You can form new views on life through security. You will feel mighty knowing your prised possesions are safe and loaded at home. you are only one application away from the best knowledge in cyber security. prepare for challenges that we will help you overcome, better online safety and relaxed environment are waiting. You can bet on it that you will learn about a jaw-dropping topic and get motivation to secure your life in the IT world. Meet the MostarTurtles and pick up the knowledge in the best way. Spend magical and warm nights in the company of BESTies under the Old Bridge with a better vision for your life. Enjoy the rich flavoured Bosnian food and culture everyday and every night.

About BEST

BEST, Board of European Students of Technology is a constantly growing nonprofit and non-political organisation. Since 1989 we provide communication, co-operation and exchange possibilities for students all over Europe. 90 Local BEST Groups (LBGs) in 32 countries are creating a growing, well organised, powerful, young and innovative student network.
